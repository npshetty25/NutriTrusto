                                                     `n                                    ABSTRACT   `n                                    Abstract   `n                               `n                                    NutriTrust             is a mobile-first web application addressing two interconnected problems faced by urban consumers across India: inability to evaluate nutritional quality of packaged food at point of purchase, and lack of a system to manage perishable grocery inventory. By combining real-time barcode-based health ratings with an AI-powered smart pantry tracker,             NutriTrust             enables informed food choices while reducing household food waste.   `n                               `n                                    Problem Space   `n                                    Health + Waste   `n                               `n                                    Target Users   `n                                    All Urban Consumers   `n                               `n                                    Survey Size   `n                                    79 Respondents   `n                               `n                                    Phase   `n                                    Empathize & Define   `n                               `n                               `n                                    Barcode Health Scanner   `n                                    Scan barcode -> instant NutriTrust score, ingredient risk flags, diet conflict alerts, globally banned ingredient checks.   `n                                    Smart Pantry Manager   `n                                    Track groceries, monitor expiry countdowns, upload receipts, and get AI recipes before items spoil.   `n                                       🔍   `n                                       🧺   `n
